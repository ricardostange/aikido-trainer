# Aikido Trainer

## Introduction
Aikido Trainer is an innovative application designed to assist practitioners of Aikido, a modern Japanese martial art. This application provides tools and resources that enhance training, learning, and performance.

## Description
Aikido Trainer is built for Aikido enthusiasts of all levels. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn fundamental techniques or an advanced practitioner refining your skills, this app offers resources to support your journey. It includes instructional videos, technique breakdowns, and a community forum for sharing experiences and getting feedback.

## Features
- **Technique Library**: Access a wide range of Aikido techniques with detailed descriptions and instructional videos.
- **Training Log**: Keep track of your training sessions, progress, and goals.
- **Community Forum**: Connect with other Aikido practitioners, share tips, and ask questions.
- **Event Calendar**: Stay updated on Aikido seminars, competitions, and community events.
- **Resource Links**: Find additional training resources and recommended reading material.
